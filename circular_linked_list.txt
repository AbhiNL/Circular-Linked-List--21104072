Q1. While traversing a single-circular linked list, which condition establishes that the traversing element/variable has reached the first element?

Ans1) While traversing a single-circular linked list, if the traversing variable is initialized to the head pointer, 
when next pointer of the traversing variable becomes equal to the head, then the traversing variable has reached the first element.
